Chicken Marsala

Description

One of my favorite dishes to order when visiting an Italian restaurant is Chicken Marsala. The umami flavor from the mushrooms is perfectly complemented by the tanginess of the marsala wine which culminates in a truly memorable dining experience. Serve with some pasta and you have an excellent dish to share with family and friends on any occasion.

Ingredients

Yields four (4) servings

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 boneless, skinless, chicken breasts - pounded 1/4" thick
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/4 cup cooking sherry

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat.
  3. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown. Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms.
  4. Pour in wine and sherry. Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
  5. Serve on top of a plate of spaghetti and garnish with parsley.
